Value at Risk Explained
Parametric VaR

Assumes returns are normally distributed. Calculated as mean + z-score * standard deviation. Quick but may underestimate tail risk.

Historical VaR

Uses the actual percentile of historical returns. No distributional assumption. Limited by the sample period.

Monte Carlo VaR

Simulates 10,000 return scenarios using estimated mean and volatility. Flexible but depends on the assumed distribution.
